Analytics

Pages may load Google Analytics 4 (measurement ID G-HHYGP07F16) to understand aggregate traffic. IP addresses are anonymized in the GA configuration.

Consent: On first visit, a banner asks you to accept or decline analytics. Your choice is stored in localStorage (aa_research_analytics_consent). Google Consent Mode defaults to denied until you accept; declining never loads the GA script.

Analytics does not run when your browser sends Do Not Track (1 or yes) or Global Privacy Control, or via legacy window.doNotTrack / msDoNotTrack.

Google’s processing is governed by Google’s privacy policy. You can opt out via Google’s browser add-on.
