Picking the extraction LLM for LightRAG.

Eleven models measured on one corpus, one chunking, one rubric. Gemini 2.5 Flash clears every retrieval cell at roughly 3 percent of Sonnet 4.6's ingest cost. The counter-intuitive part: it wins on breadth (2x more entities per document) while its relation-to-entity ratio is lower. Why breadth dominates density for graph-traversal retrieval, what local open-source can and cannot do, and what happens when the wrong model gets wired into a workstation config.
